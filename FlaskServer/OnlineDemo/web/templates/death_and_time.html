Death is a topic we barely talk about or are willing to explore in our daily life. But it is the ultimate end for every single one of us. The plots here show the main death causes by age of US residents and how you would be spending your time left. We don't want you to be scared by looking at the data; instead, we want you to realize how precious your time is and be able to cherish every moment of your life.
